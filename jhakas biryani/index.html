Welcome to Jhakas biryani

Jhakas biryani is the latest addition to the culinary horizon of fine-dining in Doha and Karachi, offering genuine Pakistani cuisine to those who appreciate elegance, refinement and good taste, hence the name. We invite you to an entirely new dining experience which shall not only be a treat for your taste buds but a journey through the glorious heritage and culture of the sub-continent. At JB we offer a number of delectable curries, Clay oven baking, Tak-a-tak and charcoal Barbecue prepared right before your eyes, not only that each recipe can be moulded and prepared according to your taste and palate.
